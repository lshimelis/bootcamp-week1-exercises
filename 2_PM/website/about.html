What even is Astrophotography?

Astrophotography is the photography of astronomical bodies such as stars, planets, and celestial events. This term covers many different types of photography including

  • Night Sky Photography:

    self explanatory in that it is simply capturing imagaes of the sky at night when celestial bodies are apparent.

  • Piggyback Astrophotography:

    involves attaching a telescope to the camera to track the movement of celestial bodies.

  • Afocal Photography:

    when the camera is placed over another eyepiece, allowing the photographer to shoot up-close, detailed images of a celestial bodies.

Astrophotography can be for anyone if you are willing to put in the energy (and miss some nights of sleep).
